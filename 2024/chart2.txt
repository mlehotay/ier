### Definitions of Cognitive Abilities, Attributes, and Life:

- **Non-living Entities**:
  - **Classification**: Non-living
  - **Definition**: Lacks biological processes, consciousness, and the ability to respond to stimuli or interact with the environment.
  - **Life**: No

- **Simple Information Processing**:
  - **Classification**: Basic Cognitive Abilities
  - **Definition**: Involves basic information processing and responsiveness to environmental stimuli.
  - **Life**: Yes

- **Basic Adaptation and Response**:
  - **Classification**: Intermediate Cognitive Abilities
  - **Definition**: Demonstrates basic adaptation, learning, and response to changes in the environment or inputs.
  - **Life**: Yes

- **Complex Interaction and Behavior**:
  - **Classification**: Advanced Cognitive Abilities
  - **Definition**: Exhibits complex interactions, behaviors, and responses to stimuli and environmental challenges.
  - **Life**: Yes

- **Sensation, Emotion, and Social Interaction**:
  - **Classification**: Advanced Cognitive Abilities
  - **Definition**: Displays sensations, emotions, social interactions, and more advanced cognitive functions.
  - **Life**: Yes

- **Self-awareness, Problem Solving, Creativity**:
  - **Classification**: Highly Developed Cognitive Abilities
  - **Definition**: Possesses self-awareness, problem-solving abilities, creativity, and more sophisticated cognitive functions.
  - **Life**: Yes

- **Reasoning, Abstract Thinking, Consciousness**:
  - **Classification**: Human-Level Cognitive Abilities
  - **Definition**: Demonstrates reasoning, abstract thinking, consciousness, and the highest level of cognitive capabilities.
  - **Life**: Yes

### Implications:

This integrated chart offers a comprehensive framework for understanding and categorizing cognitive abilities, self-awareness, consciousness, sentience, intelligence, and persistence across biological, machine, and virtual systems based on system complexity, level of consciousness, and life. It highlights the diversity, continuum, and interconnectedness of cognitive capabilities and life in nature, technology, and virtual environments, fostering interdisciplinary research, exploration, and understanding of intelligence, consciousness, life, and their various forms and manifestations.

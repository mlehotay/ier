## Reading the Map (Rules of Interpretation)

### Authority Flow

* **Downward arrows = dependence**
* Nothing flows *upward* into the Specification
* No lateral document can override another

### Hard Constraints

* ❌ No document defines experience except **IER-specification.md**
* ❌ No document detects, measures, or certifies experience
* ❌ No application is evidence *for* IER

### Allowed Uses

* Theory explains **why**
* Dynamics explains **how**
* Failure modes explain **what breaks**
* Ethics explains **what follows**
* Math explains **how to formalize**
* Meaning explains **how it appears**
